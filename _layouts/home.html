Jancy Ling Liu

Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at the College of Wooster. I received my Ph.D. from the School of Economics at the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2024.

My work focuses on the socio-economic effects of climate change and energy transitions. Using experimental and econometric methods, I study how major environmental shifts and policy influence social dynamics, including migration patterns, health outcomes, consumer behavior, community impacts, and environmental justice.

Research fields: energy and environmental economics, applied microeconomics, urban economics, and experimental economics.

You can contact me at jliu@wooster.edu.
